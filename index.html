Biography

I am Sultan S. Alqahtani, currently working as Assistant Professor at Imam University,Riyadh, Saudi Arabia and Chairman of Computer Science Department. In August 2018, I got my PhD in Computer Science (Major. Software Engineering) from Concordia University (one of the top 100 universities under 50 years in the world) under the supervision of Juergen Rilling.

My research as a former member of the Ambient Software Engineering Group (ASEG) was mainly focused on improving and supporting software engineering tasks using semantic analysis of software security vulnerabilities through semantic web techniques. You may find more information about my research on this page.

For more information you can download my Curriculum Vitae or visit my LinkedIn page. I am always looking for collaborators to work with. If you have comments, questions or suggestions, please drop me an email.
